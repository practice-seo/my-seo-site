Fitness for Beginners

Welcome to my fitness website! This guide will help beginners start exercising safely and effectively at home.

Why Fitness Matters

Regular exercise improves your health, mood, and energy. You don't need fancy equipment to get started.

Simple Beginner Workout Routine

  • 5 minutes warm-up (march in place)
  • 10 squats
  • 10 wall push-ups
  • 15 lunges
  • 30-second plank
  • Cool down and stretch
